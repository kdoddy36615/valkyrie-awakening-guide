Unlike some other recent classes such as woosa or maegu, valk awakening doesnt have some kind of infinite fixed combo rotation since most of the best skills have long CDs and you will need to alternate those with other filler skills that arent as powerful and change the order of those to extract all the efficiency of the awakening kit. Designing an 'infinite' fixed combo for the most demanding spots like hexe, quint hill, oluns, or dehkia ones for permanent DPS is impossible, and you will have to use your muscle memory to combine our best skills from the kit with the fillers that we have on an efficient way and always on a different order based on the cooldowns.

## AWAKENING KIT EXPLANATION

:SacrumFerit:
Usage: Crit buff, Frontal DMG, Importance 10/10
5s 80% crit buff with 5s cd, you want to utilize its cooldown to maximum
Recommended usage conditions: -

:FlowLucemFluxum:
Usage: Filler, Frontal DMG, Importance 2/10
Does close to no DMG. Recommended to lock if you're new to the class.
Recommended usage conditions: Can only be used after 3rd :SacrumFerit: hit or off hotbar.

:Purificatione:
Usage: Debuff, Frontal DMG, Heal, Aggro tool, Importance 9/10
10s DP debuff on 6s cd
Recommended usage conditions: Cast only with :Promptness: cancel

:Castigatio:
Usage: Frontal DMG, Importance 9/10
Main DPS tool, very low cooldown
Recommended usage conditions: -

:Hastiludium:
Usage: Movement, Filler, Frontal DMG, Importance 4/10
Recommended usage conditions: Use to travel larger distances or cast only after :BlitzStab:

:DivinaInpulsa:
Usage: Frontal DMG, Importance 6/10
Fairly low cd
Recommended usage conditions: Cast only after stab skills ( :BlitzStab: , :Hastiludium: , :SacrumFerit: ) or :Promptness:

:VerdictLanciaIustitiae:
Usage: AOE DMG, Importance 10/10
Long animation heavy hitter with decent AOE. Can cancel animation with :FlowDivinaVult: (LMB) or :PrimeSanctitasDeEnslar: (F).
Should you cancel? Want DPS? > DON'T CANCEL, Want freedom of actions > CANCEL
Recommended usage conditions: -

:SanctitasdeEnslar:
Usage: Pre-buff, Movement, Importance 10/10
10s AP buff on 9s cooldown, medium range jump
Recommended usage conditions: -

:FlowDivinaVult:
Usage: Cancel to Sanctitas, AOE DMG, Eva debuff, Importance 10/10
Recommended usage conditions: LMB always Right after :SanctitasdeEnslar:

:BlitzStab:
Usage: Frontal DMG, Importance 7/10
Recommended usage conditions: Just hit something to avoid 2nd cast

:Promptness:
Usage: Frontal DMG, Movement, Importance 8/10
Low cooldown DPS tool
Recommended usage conditions: Space after any combat action.

:TerraSancta:
Usage: Frontal DMG, Importance 5/10
Long cooldown, moderate DMG
Recommended usage conditions: Cast only after :Castigatio: or Q block (worse option)

:WaveOfLight:
Usage: Vaccum, worse DP debuff, Importance 1/10
Situational
Recommended usage conditions: Cancel with Q block of :DeathLineChase: backwards.

:HeavensEcho:
Usage: Pre-buff, Importance 10/10
1 min DP and accuracy buff
Recommended usage conditions: Space during Q block with your Lancia

Core skill: Castigatio :Castigatio:

## PRE AWAKENING

Since just awakening kit is by far not enough to keep up with spots beyond midgame, you will be forced to use your longsword a lot. You want to fit the fillers one by one between your awakening kit, do not just use all of them one after another.

:GladiusGloriae:
Usage: Frontal DMG, Importance 7/10
Long cooldown decent DMG skill
Recommended usage conditions: After :ShieldChase:

:SeveringLight:
Usage: Filler, Frontal DMG, Importance 3/10
Recommended usage conditions: After :GladiusGloriae:

:CelestialSpear:
Usage: Ranged DMG, Crit Buff, Aggro tool, Importance 7/10
Recommended usage conditions: -

:Counter: + :ShieldThrow: + :CelestialSpear: cancel
A cancel worth learning, Importance 4/10
Recommended usage conditions: :Counter: after :ShieldChase: into :ShieldThrow: into :CelestialSpear: into :ShieldThrow:
