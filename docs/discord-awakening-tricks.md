## Divina Inpulsa
:DivinaInpulsa: is an animation that stabs twice. It can be canceled and can cancel other abilities.

**Pre-Cancels:** :BlitzStab:, :UltimateBlitzStab:, :Hastiludium:, :SacrumFerit:, :Promptness:, Lateral :LanciaTraining:

## Shield Throw Canceling
**Pre-Cancels:** :CelestialSpear:, :CelestialSmite:, :Counter:, and :FlyingKick: if you hit a target.
**Post-Cancels:** :CelestialSpear:, :CelestialSmite:, :FlyingKick:, :ShieldChase:, Sidestep (A/D + RMB)
Example: https://youtu.be/Iq1lCjY3dBc

## Q/Block Canceling
Most of the awakening skills can be canceled through blocking.
**Protected:** :BlitzStab:, :UltimateBlitzStab:, :DivinaInpulsa:, :VerdictLanciaIustitiae:, :FlowRevelationSanctus:
**Unprotected:**
- Quick Terra (:Promptness: > Q > :TerraSancta:)
- Quick Grab (:Promptness: > Q > :Punishment:)
- Quick Vacuum (:WaveOfLight: > Q)
Comparison — No Cancel: https://youtu.be/J1faJyof2aE  · With Cancel: https://youtu.be/UIeLTJDMLxo

## Sanctitas de Enslar
Many uses: engaging, disengaging, iframing, and SA damage.
- **Protected Transition:** register :SanctitasdeEnslar: to a quickslot to use it from the pre-awakening state.
- **Flow: Enslar's Divinity:** LMB after :SanctitasdeEnslar: casts the flow (:FlowDivinaVult:). Completely unprotected, subpar damage; cancel during the skill with Q.
- You are NOT able to use the side-step (A/D+LMB) after :SanctitasdeEnslar: — the flow is prioritized over the side-step.
- **Bombing with 100%:** use :WaveOfLight: before :SanctitasdeEnslar:; maintain your Critical Rate buff with :SacrumFerit:, :CelestialSpear:, or :CelestialSmite:.

## Death Line Chase
:DeathLineChase: moves forward while maintaining Forward Guard + Super Armor via Shift + W/S.
- **Backwards Dash Bug:** if your block is getting hit while doing this even with a :ShieldChase: off-cooldown, it will continue using :DeathLineChase: (unprotected while off-cooldown). You can get caught in most situations — be aware.
- **LMB Flow:** pressing LMB directly after :DeathLineChase: performs a float CC. This consumes the :Promptness: cooldown.

## Important skill buffs
Only the most important buffs; each skill may have more (read the tooltips).
- :HeavensEcho: **Heaven's Echo:** All DP +25 to yourself, +15% Accuracy Rate to self and nearby allies for 60s. SHIFT+Q in pre-awak, SPACE while blocking in awak.
- :BreathofElion: **Breath of Elion:** Super Armor to yourself and allies for 3s when used with your lancia.
- :ElionsBlessing: **Elion's Blessing:** All Damage Reduction +50% and +100% all-debuff resistance to self and nearby allies for 6s. Limited max resistance against opponent's debuffs.
- :CelestialCry: **Celestial Cry:** All DP +20 and +12% Accuracy Rate to self and allies for 60s; Magic DP +15 and Movement Speed +20% for 10s. All-DP and Accuracy buff does NOT stack with Heaven's Echo.
- :DivineDescent: **Divine Descent:** All DP +10 and all Special Attack damage +5% to self and allies for 10s. All-DP buff stacks with Heaven's Echo and/or Celestial Cry.
- :CelestialSpear: :CelestialSmite: :SacrumFerit: **Critical Hit Rate buffs:** Critical Hit Rate +80% for 5s.

## Quickslots
**Required**
- :FlowLucemFluxum: Extremely useful skill for comboing
- :ElionsBlessing: Our PA, can only be cast from quickslot
- :NobleSpirit: Our E-buff, can only be cast from quickslot

**Important**
- :JustCounter: Useful for situations where you can't put up block first
- :SanctitasdeEnslar: Protected swap from quickslot; also prevents accidentally using it after :WaveOfLight:
- :CelestialCry: :CelestialSmite: :DivineDescent: :DivineSlam: Slower than the input; can be quickslotted for skill cooldown tracking

**Quality of Life**
- :DivineJudgmentofLight: For whenever Celestial Spear/Smite is on cooldown. Can be used from awakening
- :ShiningJudgmentofLight: When you instantly need some protected movement. Can be used from awakening
- :WaveOfLight: Prevents accidentally using :SanctitasdeEnslar: afterwards

**Additional / Preference**
- :ShiningDash: Useful for chasing people in duels, can only be cast from quickslot
- :Punishment: For skill cooldown tracking
- :SkywardStrike: CC option, niche skill
- :GladiusGloriae: Best used if charge up is cancelled; can be quickslotted for skill cooldown tracking

## Useful cancels
**:VerdictLanciaIustitiae: Verdict cancels:** LMB, LMB ➔ Q, A/D + LMB, :SanctitasdeEnslar:
Biggest issue after recent changes: Verdict animation-locks once you start pulling the lancia out of the ground (worse with casting-speed debuffs). Cancel before this happens; with :SanctitasdeEnslar: you can cancel at any time during the animation by pressing F.

**:GladiusGloriae: Gladius cancels:** :SharpLight: :JustCounter: :ShieldChase: :SeveringLight: :DivineSlam: :DivineDescent: :SidewaysCut: :RighteousCharge: :Counter: :ForwardSlash:; :CelestialSpear: :CelestialSmite: :CelestialCry: if :SanctitasdeEnslar: is on cd.
The first 3 (Sharp Light, Just Counter, Shield Chase) are the most useful; the rest is for knowledge.

## Movement combos & clips
:Hastiludium: is now one of our main movement skills; a few other options also work in PvP.
- **Old school:** SHIFT + W ➔ LMB ➔ SPACE 🔄
- **Shield Chase dash:** SHIFT + W ➔ SPACE + Q ➔ C ➔ SPACE + LMB 🔄
- **Promptness Walk:** Hold SPACE, alternate between Q and LMB
- **Backwards DLC:** Hold SHIFT + S + LMB

### Lateral movement
**Awakening**
- **Side-step** (:LanciaTraining:): Q + A/D + LMB. +20% movement speed on a lateral LMB (A/D + LMB); partial i-frame, can dodge grabs.
- **Crab Walk** (:SacrumFerit:): Q + A/D + RMB. +80% crit hit rate on skill use (A/D + RMB); frontal guard off cooldown; nice for zero-stamina movement. Remember to press Q after.

**Pre-awakening**
- **Side-step** (:sidestep:): A/D + LMB + RMB. Press A or D and hold LMB + RMB; may bug out with :SeveringLight: unlocked. Our best side-step i-frame.

## Hastiludium
7s cooldown; travels far and quickly; applies a 30% knockdown-resistance debuff on hits.
- **Charge-up cancel:** :DeathLineChase: SHIFT + W ➔ :Hastiludium: W + F
- Charges as you use it; the last hit is extremely powerful and can one-hit squishier enemies. The longer the dash, the greater the stab damage.
- The core version adds a knockdown — useful for a small-scale pre-engage.

## Transitions
**Pre-awake ➔ Awake**
- :SanctitasdeEnslar: QUICKSLOT ONLY [I-frame into SA]
- :valkcswap: C [FG]
- :BlitzStab: W + RMB
- Notable skills to use before Promptness to swap into awake:
  - :sidestep: A/D + RMB ➔ :Promptness: SPACE [Partial I-frame into SA]
  - :SidewaysCut: A/D + LMB ➔ :Promptness: SPACE [Very quick cancel]
  - :ShieldChase: SHIFT + A/D/S ➔ :Promptness: SPACE [SA+FG into SA]
  - :JustCounter: W + LMB ➔ :Promptness: SPACE [FG into SA]
