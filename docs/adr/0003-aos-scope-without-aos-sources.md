# AOS is the only PvP scope, drawn from the guide's 1v1 and Large-Scale content

PvP scope is **AOS (Arena of Solare, capped 3v3) only**. The catch: the guide contains **no
AOS-specific content** — its PvP is organized as **1v1** and **Large-Scale**. We treat *both*
as AOS-relevant inputs: 1v1 supplies grab/CC/burst, and Large-Scale supplies the
**protected-ability (SA/FG) emphasis** that capped 3v3 actually rewards. Combos are tagged
`scale: 1v1 | large-scale` for filtering, but both sit under the PvP/AOS umbrella — Large-Scale
is **not** flagged out-of-scope (an earlier draft did this; reversed after the user noted AOS
leans on protected play).

A future reader will wonder why 1v1/large-scale content is labeled AOS and why there are no
"real" AOS combos in v1: because the source has none. We deliberately do **not** source the
Valkyrie Discord or anything external for v1 (faithful-to-sheet, per ADR 0001). The dedicated
AOS layer is **post-v1**, built from Rank-1 replay observations the user will capture, dropping
into the reserved R1 AOS route with no rework.
