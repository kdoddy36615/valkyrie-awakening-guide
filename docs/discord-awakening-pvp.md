## GENERAL INFO

Awakening Valkyrie's combo are inherently unprotected, most of the combo listed below are designed for smallscale.
If you're looking for protected combo go to the :Guard: Protected Combos section or refer to the Valkyrie Guide for large scale oriented combos.

Combo theory for Awakening Valkyrie.

### Catches
Pre-awak
:Punishment: (E) works in Awakening
:CelestialSpear: (S+E) works in Awakening
:JustCounter: (W+LMB) from :Guard: only, HOTBAR otherwise
:Counter: (S+LMB)
:GladiusGloriae: (F) Gladius Gloriae Cancels
Awakening
:WaveOfLight: (S+F)
:BlitzStab: (W+RMB)
Core: :SanctitasdeEnslar: (F) | :Hastiludium: (W+F) | :SacrumFerit: (RMB) | :Castigatio: (S+LMB)

### Pre-buffs
Critical hit rate: :SacrumFerit: (RMB) | :CelestialSpear: (S+E)
Melee AP: :DivinaInpulsa: (Shift+Q) | :SanctitasdeEnslar: (F)

### De-buffs
All DP: :Purificatione: (Shift+LMB)
All Evasion: :BlitzStab: (W+RMB) | :FlowDivinaVult: (F/LMB after :SanctitasdeEnslar: & LMB after :VerdictLanciaIustitiae:)

### Re-CCs
Pre-awak: :SharpLight: (Shift+LMB) | :GladiusGloriae: (F)
Awak: :FlowLucemFluxum: (Hotbar) | :BlitzStab: (W+RMB)
Core: :Hastiludium: (W+F) | :SanctitasdeEnslar: (F)

### Typical Payload
:Castigatio: :TerraSancta: :BlitzStab:
S+LMB -> Hold -> W+RMB

### Down Smash fishing
Priority top to bottom
:DivinaInpulsa: (Shift+Q)
:DivinePower: (Shift+F)
:DivineJudgmentofLight: (S+E -> LMB) needs :CelestialSpear: off CD
:DivineSlam: (Shift+Z)

### When should you use :Purificatione:?
Instantly after a :Punishment: with :Promptness: -> :Purificatione:
Pros: Apply All DP debuff early; Air Attack modifier utilized
Cons: No Critical Hit Rate outside of innate 18% (or 48% with Critical Hit Rate addon) unless prebuffed; Needs a grab catch

After the re-cc
Pros: Most likely 100% Critical Hit Rate; Air Attack modifier utilized
Cons: Applies All DP debuff later in the combo; Air Attack only works after :FlowLucemFluxum:( :Promptness:) / :SharpLight::Promptness:

At any other point
Pros: Most likely 100% Critical Hit Rate
Cons: No Air Attack modifier

## PROTECTED COMBOS
Sarron [FAT], Moderator — 3/14/25, 5:18 PM

General note for all protected combos using these:
:TerraSancta: > :Guard: > :VerdictLanciaIustitiae:
or
:FlowDivinaVult: > :Guard: > :TerraSancta:
Holding S+Q during :TerraSancta: (S+RMB) or :FlowDivinaVult: (F/LMB) will minimize the delay at the end without canceling hits. Make sure to press "S" first.
I would advise casting :VerdictLanciaIustitiae: from hotbar. You can spam the hotkey while holding S+Q and it will instantly cast after entering :Guard:.

## FINISHERS
Sarron [FAT], Moderator — 3/17/25, 11:55 AM
Every combo ending with :BlitzStab: or :BlitzStab: :DivinaInpulsa: can be extended with one of these finishers. Keep in mind, your target will get up during the finisher unless you pull off a reset.

Option 1:
:DivinePower: :CelestialSpear: :DivineJudgmentofLight:
(Shift+F -> S+E -> LMB)
Go to if you're trying to reset, good damage (unprotected/possible reset)

Option 2:
:ShieldChase: :GladiusGloriae: :CelestialSpear: :DivineJudgmentofLight:
(Shift+A/D -> F -> S+E -> LMB)
Highest damage, gain some distance to finish off your target (unprotected/no possible reset)

Option 3:
:VerdictLanciaIustitiae: :SanctitasdeEnslar: :FlowDivinaVult:
(Shift+RMB -> F -> F)
Lower damage, somewhat vulnerable to grab but can iframe them or go behind frontal guards with :SanctitasdeEnslar: (fully protected/no possible reset)
